WE ARE UNC PHOTO CLUB

Who we are:

We are a student-led organization motivated to capture life in every moment through your lens. We are inspired by student photographers whom we come in contact with everyday. Join us for our bi-weekly meetings, inspirational speakers, captivating workshops, and wholesome photo-walks.

Meet the Team:

Emma is a current Sophomore studying Computer Science and Journalism. She is from Brevard, NC, a small mountain town. She enjoys photographing landscapes and portraits.
